Save Water with These Tips

Install low-flow showerheads, faucets, and toilets to significantly reduce water usage without sacrificing performance.

Even a one- or two-minute reduction in your shower time can save up to 150 gallons of water per month.

When brushing your teeth or shaving, turn off the tap instead of letting it run.

Water your garden during the early morning or late evening to minimize evaporation.

Only run your dishwasher and washing machine when they are full to optimize water use per wash cycle.

Install a rain barrel to collect water from your roof to use in your garden. This conserves water and reduces runoff pollution.

Opt for native plants that require less water, fertilizers, and pesticides. These plants thrive in your local climate with minimal watering.

Share water-saving tips with family and friends to increase the impact of conservation efforts.

Water is a precious resource essential for life, yet many regions, including Morocco, face significant water scarcity. Adopting water-saving practices is crucial to ensure sustainable water management for future generations.
